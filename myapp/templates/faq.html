Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions and learn how to use NotesApp effectively.

How do I create a new note?

Once logged in, click the "Add Note" button on the main dashboard. You can add a title, write your content using our rich text editor, assign a category, and even attach files.

Can I organize my notes into categories?

Yes! You can create custom categories with specific colors from the sidebar. When creating or editing a note, simply select the relevant category to keep your workspace organized.

How do I favorite a note?

On any note card in your dashboard, click the heart icon. This will mark the note as a favorite. You can then quickly filter all your favorite notes using the toggle on the dashboard.

Is there a limit to how many files I can attach?

Currently, you can attach one file per note (PDF, image, etc.). If you need to attach more documents, we recommend creating a ZIP file or linking to an external drive.
